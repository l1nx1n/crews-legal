Frequently asked questions

How do I sign up?
Enter your phone number and we'll text you a 6-digit code. Type it in, add your name and an optional photo, and you're in. No passwords, no email required.
I didn't get my verification code
Codes usually arrive within a few seconds. If not: check the number you entered (including country code), wait 60 seconds, and tap resend. If you're on a restricted or corporate network, try switching to Wi-Fi or cellular data — some networks block the delivery.
How do I add friends to my crew?
Go to the Friends tab. Search by phone number, or connect your contacts to see which of them are already on Crew and add them with one tap. Adding someone is instant — they don't have to approve — and they'll see the plans you broadcast going forward.
How do I share a plan?
Tap the green + button, add what you're doing, a time, and a place, then broadcast it. Everyone in your crew sees it in their feed and can tap "I'm in!" or "Interested."
How do RSVPs and the "network effect" work?
When a friend RSVPs to a plan, that plan can also appear for their crew — so good plans spread one degree out. You always see who's going before you commit. Hosts can turn this off per-plan when creating it.
Can I control who sees my plans?
Yes. When creating a plan you can exclude specific people from that broadcast. You can also mute a noisy friend so their plans don't notify you, or block someone entirely. Your feed is yours.
How do I delete my account?
Open your Profile (tap your photo, top-right of Home) → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, activities, crew connections, and photo from our systems.
Is my information private?
Your plans are only visible to your crew and, via the network effect, their crews — never publicly. There are no public profiles, no discovery beyond your phone contacts, and no ads. See our privacy policy below for details.
