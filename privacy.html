Crews - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Crews, we collect:

  • Phone number: Used for account creation and login via SMS verification.
  • Name: Your first and last name, displayed to other users.
  • Profile photo: Optional, stored securely in cloud storage.
  • Contacts: If you grant permission, we access your phone contacts to help you find friends on Crews. Contact data is used only for matching and is not stored on our servers beyond what is necessary for the friend-finding feature.
  • Activity data: Activities you create, RSVPs, and crew memberships.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To show your activities to your crew and their connections (network effect).
  • To send you in-app notifications about crew activity.
  • To send one-time SMS invitations to contacts you choose to invite.

3. SMS Messages

Crews sends SMS messages only when a user explicitly invites a contact to join the app. Recipients receive a single invitation message. Recipients can reply STOP at any time to opt out of future messages. Message and data rates may apply. We do not share opt-in data with third parties, and mobile opt-in consent is never shared with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google): Phone authentication.
  • Supabase: Database and file storage.
  • Twilio: SMS delivery for invitations.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS), secure authentication tokens, and access-controlled databases to protect your data.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the app Profile settings. This permanently removes your user data, activities, crew memberships, and profile photo from our systems.

7. Children's Privacy

Crews is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@crewsapp.com.
